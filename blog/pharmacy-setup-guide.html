2. Plan Your Pharmacy Setup

A successful pharmacy requires proper layout planning, storage systems, workflow management and operational processes. Professional guidance can help avoid common mistakes.

3. Focus on Inventory Management

Effective inventory management helps reduce wastage, improve availability of medicines and increase profitability.

4. Train Pharmacy Staff

Well-trained staff improve customer service, workflow efficiency and overall pharmacy performance.

5. Grow Your Pharmacy Business

Continuous improvement, better systems and customer-focused services help pharmacies achieve sustainable growth.
